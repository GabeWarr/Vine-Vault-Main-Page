How To Move
Push your arms as if you're crawling, not pushing downwards.
*Use your left hand forward and pull backward, then do the same with your right arm.*

In This Case:

  1. You Can Walk Properly.
  2. You Learned How To Jump In Moments You Need To.

Put a hand or two on a wall and push against it. The walls let you stay on them for a moment before bouncing.
*It's like bouncing on a wall like a rubber ball!*

In This Case:
